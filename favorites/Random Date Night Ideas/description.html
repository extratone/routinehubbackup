Description

Randomly selects a date night idea. You can choose any date, or outdoor vs indoor (you may notice we live in the rural intermountain west). Options to share via text and clipboard, try again or see the entire list. You could automate it and get an idea every Thursday evening.

Feel free to modify the date ideas. The ‘See Full List’ options should automatically adjust.
