Description

This is a handy utility for any wristwatch enthusiast. It determines your mechanical watches daily drift, particularly under wearing conditions, hassle-free and accurately.

The data gained allows for simple regulation of a wristwatch according to its actual everyday performance rather than by somewhat theoretical desktop testing results. To use the shortcut:

• Simply take two snapshots of your watch dial casually during your day.

• Enter the dial readings and have the shortcut calculate your watches daily rate from the photos’ metadata.

Detailed info is included in the shortcut.
