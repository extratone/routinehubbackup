Description

Handoff Music is a Shortcut for iOS 15 that lets you transfer the currently playing song and your entire Music queue to your other iOS or iPadOS devices (macOS and watchOS untested)

How to use: 1. Take a screenshot of your Up Next Queue on the device you’d like to Handoff from, and crop out all the text other than the song and artist names. 3. Tap the share button from the screenshot editor 4. Find Handoff Music and select it. As long as both devices have an internet connection and are signed into the same iCloud account, syncing should take just a few seconds. 5. On the receiving device, run Handoff Music with no input. Your music will begin playing immediately, and your queue will appear as well. (For iOS 14 devices, you can receive the queue — but not send it — using the companion Shortcut Receive Music Handoff.)
