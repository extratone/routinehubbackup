Description

Convert nearly any audio format to MP3.

Hi there, I made a shortcut to convert audio to MP3.

You’ll have to sign up for your own account and API key at CloudConvert.com but it’s free and pretty easy.

Plug it in the text box and open the MP3 Converter shortcut from the Share menu and it will prompt you for a download location when ready.

In putting this together, I didn’t find anything very similar so I thought I’d share :)
