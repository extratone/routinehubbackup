Description

This shortcut let’s you use WidgetPack to create beautiful iOS 14 widgets showing the covers of your favorite albums. When tapped, they can launch a shortcut that plays or resumes your favorite album.

It works with whatever music service you like, as long as they use deep or universal links (the ones that don’t open Safari first)
