Edit: here are some more films (Blade Runners new and old, BlacKkKlansmen, Finding Nemo, Macbeth, Skyfall, Goldfinger

You have a choice of three visualisations:

  • Filmstrip

  • Barcode (written by /u/MatthewMK7)

  • Grid

The individual visualisations are output to iCloud along with a black and white version of the combined output.

Please note that the 'Grid' visualisation won't work for videos under around 3 mins.

Many thanks to /u/MatthewMK7 for the idea and for use of his awesome barcode shortcut!
