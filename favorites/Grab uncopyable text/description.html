Description

I often come across text on my iPhone or iPad that I can’t copy, such as error messages, screenshots or PDFs.

For the Mac there is an inexpensive tool that makes such texts copyable: it's called TextSniper and costs $ 6.00 one-time (currently reduced to $ 3.99). In this tool, you simply select the section that you want to be translated into copyable text and then the software does the rest of the work.

Here is a shortcut that lets you grab uncopyable text that’s on your display and save it as plain text to the clipboard.
