0.4 2 years, 2 months ago
iOS 14
This version makes several improvements, but it also introduces a couple of annoying visual bugs. If you know your way around shortcuts, especially if you have experience with WidgetPack, please take a look at this and see if you can make it stop squishing photos, and/or if you can find a way to make the photos and names align closer to the bottom of the widget. I think they might be WidgetPack bugs, but I could be wrong.

Here’s what’s new:
1. The shortcut now allows you to select multiple contacts from the beginning (it needs to be 4 contacts for now)
2. First names of contacts now appear below their contact image
3. Contacts with no image can be added, and appear as a blank gray circle
4. The widget comes in 2 designs — one styled to match the Phone app, and the other in white or black with a Phone icon.
5. Contacts with multiple numbers may cause the wrong phone number to be launched for other contacts on the widget. Weird.
