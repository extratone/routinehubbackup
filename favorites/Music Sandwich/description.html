Description

This shortcut is guaranteed to take your music experience to a whole new level !!!

It's an easy-to-use shortcut, which enables the user to:

  1. Get cool insightful statistics about the song they are playing,
  2. Add the song playing to a specific playlist, without creating duplicates
  3. Shuffle and play songs from the library or a specific playlist
  4. Play top songs from a specific period
  5. Discover forgotten gems that might not have been played or were least played
  6. Play new songs from the library
  7. Rewind songs from the present to a specific time in the past

You surely don't wanna be left out on this train, so go on and enjoy the NEW experience with this shortcut

To set up the shortcut, you are strongly recommended to watch the following short video from YouTube
