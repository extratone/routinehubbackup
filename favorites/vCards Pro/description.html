Description

vCards Pro is a vCard management tool. vCards are great for creating visually-appealing menus in Shortcuts. However, managing them can be difficult, cumbersome, and time-consuming.

So, I created vCards Pro.

vCards Pro is the quickest and most efficient way to manage your vCards in Shortcuts.
