English Description

What to Buy is a shopping list shortcut where you can have as many shopping lists as you want, add or remove items to them, edit the items, mark them as completed and lots of other things.

You can update using UpdateKit (Run What to Buy X shortcut, click in "More options" and click in "Update What to Buy X")

You can find older versions and the .shortcut or .wflow files of all versions on my Google Drive. Click here

You can find more info on our website (click here)

You will select your language at the first run of the shortcut

You can create your own plugins to What to Buy. Click here for more info
